Our Clinic Services

Initial Consultation and Diagnosis

Our initial consultation and diagnostic services are designed to thoroughly assess your knee condition. Our specialists successfully use advanced imaging techniques such as X-rays, MRIs, and CT scans to provide accurate diagnoses. We take the time to understand your symptoms and medical history to develop a personalized treatment plan. Trust us to identify the root cause of your knee issues and recommend the best course of action.

Arthroscopic Surgery

Arthroscopic surgery is a minimally invasive procedure that allows our surgeons to diagnose and treat knee joint issues. Using small incisions and specialized instruments, we can address problems such as meniscus tears and ligament injuries. This technique offers quicker recovery times and less postoperative pain compared to traditional surgery. Our experienced surgeons are dedicated to providing you with the best possible outcomes.

Knee Replacement

Knee replacement surgery, also known as arthroplasty, is a highly effective procedure for relieving pain and restoring function in damaged knees. Our surgeons use state-of-the-art implants to replace the damaged joint surfaces. This surgery is typically recommended for patients with severe arthritis or significant knee injuries. With proper rehabilitation, you can expect to regain mobility and improve your quality of life.

Ligament Reconstruction

Ligament reconstruction surgery is vitally crucial for patients with torn ACL or PCL ligaments. Our skilled surgeons use grafts to reconstruct the damaged ligaments, ensuring stability and strength in the knee joint. This procedure is often performed arthroscopically, resulting in less scarring and faster recovery. Post-surgery, our modern rehabilitation programs help you regain your strength and prevent any future injuries.

Physical Therapy and Rehabilitation

Physical therapy and rehabilitation are integral cornerstones and essential components of knee treatment. Our therapists design personalized exercise programs to improve strength, flexibility, and function. We use a combination of manual therapy, therapeutic exercises, and modalities to aid recovery. Regular physical therapy sessions can help you achieve optimal results and prevent future knee problems.

Injection Procedures

Injection procedures such as hyaluronic acid, PRP, and corticosteroids offer non-surgical options for managing knee pain. Hyaluronic acid injections provide lubrication and cushioning in the joint. PRP injections use your own blood to promote healing and reduce inflammation. Corticosteroid injections are effective for reducing pain and swelling. These treatments can delay or even prevent the need for surgery.
