Our Journey

Our story began ten years ago when a group of passionate orthopedic surgeons came together with a vision: to create a clinic that provides world-class knee surgery and joint treatments to the people of Newport and beyond. Since our inception, we have been dedicated to improving the lives of our patients by offering cutting-edge medical care in a compassionate and welcoming environment.

Our Commitment to Quality

Over the years, we have grown and evolved, but our commitment to quality has remained steadfast. We believe that excellent medical care is not just about advanced technology and skilled professionals; it is also about understanding the unique needs of each patient. That is why we tailor our treatment plans to meet the specific needs of our patients, ensuring they receive the best possible care.

Patient Satisfaction

We are proud to say that in our ten years of operation, we have not had a single dissatisfied patient. Our patients' testimonials speak volumes about the care and dedication of our staff. From the moment they walk through our doors, our patients know they are in good hands. Our friendly staff and state-of-the-art facilities ensure a comfortable and positive experience for everyone who visits our clinic.

Continuous Improvement

To maintain our high standards, our doctors undergo rigorous retraining and certification programs every year. This commitment to continuous improvement ensures that we stay at the forefront of medical advancements and provide our patients with the latest and most effective treatments. Our team of experienced surgeons and medical professionals is dedicated to achieving the best possible outcomes for our patients.

Our Community

Being a part of the Newport community is something we cherish deeply. Over the past decade, we have formed strong bonds with our patients and their families. We believe in giving back to the community that has supported us, and we regularly participate in local health fairs and educational seminars to promote awareness about knee and joint health.

Looking to the Future

As we look to the future, we remain committed to our mission of providing exceptional orthopedic care. We will continue to innovate, improve, and expand our services to meet the growing needs of our patients. We are excited about what the next decade holds and look forward to continuing our journey of excellence in healthcare.
